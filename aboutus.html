A Little About the Catatechnics

We are a FTC robotics team from Wayland Union Middle School. We make sure the students do all the real work including this website! We also make it a point that we all show gracious professionalism. Joe Harnish Senior Manager of Devops says that "FTC is a place where junior high students learn to function in a work place enviorment and as a team." He has helped and sponsored FTC for a little less than two years now with his children being participants as well.

Our Team Set Up

Our total team makeup is a little bit diffrent than most others. It consists of 5 main groups the builders, the coders, our YouTubers, the recorders, and the fundraiseres (a person may be in more than one group). The builders handle the robots physical construction they are the ones who use all the nuts and bolts. The builders are also made of two teams that each come up with a robot symetrical to the other group. The coders are the people who make and use instructions for the robot of their build team. Just like the build teams the code teams makes similar code to the opposing team but it is slightly diffrent so in the final competition we have a robot that is the best of both worlds. Our YouTubers make videos to post online so we can be better known. The recorders take notes on what we do so we can see where we went wrong if there is ever to be a problem. They also do all our reasearch. Finally, we have the fundraisers. Well, we have to get our money from somewhere and the fundraising team is in charge of just that. They raise money in several diffrent ways including fundraisers and finding sponsors and collecting their donations. And thats it for how our team is set up.
